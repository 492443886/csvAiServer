Cucumber Vodka Cooler
Ingredients:
1 ½ - 2 ounces Vodka Friday Premium vodka
3-4 slices of cucumber
1/2 ounce (15 ml) freshly squeezed lime juice
1/2 ounce (15 ml) simple syrup
Club soda or sparkling water
Cucumber slice or mint sprig (for garnish, optional)
Ice cubes

In a cocktail shaker, muddle the cucumber slices gently to release their flavor and aroma.
Add ice, vodka, lime juice and simple syrup (Simple syrup is made by dissolving equal parts of sugar and water.) Shake the mixture vigorously for about 15-20 seconds to combine the flavors and chill the ingredients. Strain into a glass with ice cubes.Top up the glass with club soda and garnish the drink with a cucumber slice or a mint sprig by placing it on the rim of the glass or dropping it into the drink.

Ice cubes

Vodka Friday Premium Vodk to the glass or shaker.Fill the glass with ice cubes.
