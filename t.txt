Orange Creamsicle Cocktail
1 1/2 oz Vodka Friday Premium
3 oz orange juice
1 oz cream or half-and-half
1/2 oz simple syrup 
Orange slice (for garnish)

Fill a shaker with ice cubes.
Add vodka, orange juice, cream (or half-and-half), and simple syrup (if desired) to the shaker.
Shake well until chilled and combined.
Strain the mixture into a glass filled with ice.
Garnish with an orange slice.
Serve and enjoy!

Vodka Watermelon Cucumber Cooler:

1 1/2 oz Vodka Friday Premium
2 oz watermelon juice
2 oz cucumber juice
1/2 oz lime juice
1/2 oz simple syrup (optional, for added sweetness)
Fresh mint leaves (for garnish)
Watermelon wedge (for garnish)
Cucumber slice (for garnish)

In a blender, combine watermelon chunks and cucumber slices. 
Blend until smooth.
Fill a shaker with ice cubes.
Add vodka, watermelon juice, cucumber juice, lime juice, and simple syrup (if desired) to the shaker. Shake well until chilled and combined.
Strain the mixture into a glass filled with ice.
Garnish with fresh mint leaves, a watermelon wedge, and a cucumber slice.
